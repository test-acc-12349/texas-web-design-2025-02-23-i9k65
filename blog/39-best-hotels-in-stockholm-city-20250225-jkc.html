39 Best Hotels in Stockholm City

Published on February 23, 2024 • 12 min read

Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, is a city that effortlessly blends history, culture, and modernity. Known for its stunning archipelago, vibrant neighborhoods, and world-class attractions, Stockholm is a must-visit destination for travelers. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, finding the perfect place to stay is essential.

Luxury Hotels in Stockholm

For those seeking opulence and unparalleled service, Stockholm offers a range of luxury hotels that redefine elegance.

1. Grand Hôtel Stockholm

Located on the waterfront, this iconic hotel has been a symbol of luxury since 1874. With its Michelin-starred restaurants, spa, and breathtaking views of the Royal Palace, it's a favorite among discerning travelers.

2. Ett Hem

This boutique hotel feels more like a private home than a traditional hotel. With only 12 rooms, Ett Hem offers personalized service, a cozy library, and a beautiful garden.

Mid-Range Hotels in Stockholm

If you're looking for comfort without breaking the bank, these mid-range hotels offer excellent value for money.

Family-Friendly Hotels

Traveling with kids? These family-friendly hotels offer amenities and services tailored to families.

Stockholm's diverse range of hotels ensures that every traveler can find the perfect place to stay. Whether you're looking for luxury, affordability, or a unique boutique experience, the city has something to offer. From historic landmarks to modern design, these 39 hotels showcase the best of Stockholm's hospitality.
