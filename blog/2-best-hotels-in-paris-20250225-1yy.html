Best Hotels in Paris

Published on February 23, 2024 • 8 min read

Paris, the city of love, light, and magnificent architecture, is a top destination for millions of tourists every year. Not surprisingly, this city is teeming with a rich array of accommodations. From luxury palaces to charming boutique hotels, Paris has it all.

1. Ritz Paris

Steeped in glamour and history, the Ritz Paris is undoubtedly one of the best hotels in the city. Located in the ritzy Place Vendome, the hotel has been serving luxury since 1898. With its opulent rooms, top-tier service, and Michelin-starred dining, the Ritz Paris is perfect for those seeking a taste of the high life. The hotel also houses the world-renowned Chanel spa, a must-visit for beauty aficionados.

2. Le Meurice

Positioned right across from the Tuileries Garden, Le Meurice boasts a fantastic location and splendid views. The hotel, a part of the Dorchester Collection, offers a seamless blend of classic 18th-century design with modern comforts. Le Meurice is also home to Alain Ducasse's two-Michelin-starred restaurant, so culinary excellence is guaranteed.

3. Hôtel Plaza Athénée

Next on our list is another gem from the Dorchester Collection, the Hôtel Plaza Athénée. Nestled on the famous Avenue Montaigne, known for high-end shopping, this hotel is well-known for its striking red geranium window displays. With its chic rooms, five dining venues, and Dior Institute spa, Hôtel Plaza Athénée exudes luxury in every sense.

4. Shangri-La Hotel, Paris

Originally the residence of Napoleon Bonaparte's grandnephew, the Shangri-La Hotel, Paris brilliantly captures the essence of French elegance. Here, you can enjoy panoramic views of the Eiffel Tower and the Seine River. For gastronomy lovers, the two-Michelin-starred Shang Palace is a delight not to be missed.

5. Le Bristol Paris

Le Bristol Paris, located on the prestigious rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, is the epitome of Parisian luxury. This hotel, known for its exquisite French garden, houses spacious and stylish rooms adorned with Louis XV and Louis XVI furniture. The culinary offerings are just as impressive, with the three-Michelin-starred restaurant Epicure, leading the way.

6. The Hoxton, Paris

If you're searching for something a little more contemporary, then The Hoxton, Paris may be the perfect fit. Located in the trendy 2nd arrondissement, this hotel is housed in an 18th-century building that blends historic charm with trendy design. The Hoxton offers chic, comfortable rooms and vibrant communal spaces, making it a hit among the fashion-forward crowd.

7. Saint James Paris

The Saint James Paris is a unique find. This hotel, set in Paris's 16th arrondissement, is housed in a former chateau. Its setting, enveloped by a private garden, offers a tranquil retreat from the city's hustle and bustle. With its whimsical decor and Michelin-starred dining, the Saint James Paris is a beautiful blend of tradition and innovation.

Each of these hotels offers something unique, from unparalleled luxury to chic contemporary design. They are more than mere accommodations; they provide an immersive Parisian experience. They serve as gateways to explore the magic that Paris beholds, thereby making your stay in this city unforgettable. Choosing any of these hotels in Paris will certainly elevate your travel experience and create lasting memories.
