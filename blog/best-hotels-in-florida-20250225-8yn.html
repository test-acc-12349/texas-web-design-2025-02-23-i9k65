Best Hotels in Florida: A Guide to Luxurious Stays

Published on February 23, 2024 • 10 min read

Florida, often referred to as the Sunshine State, is a premier destination for travelers seeking sun, sand, and world-class hospitality. Whether you're planning a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a business trip, Florida offers a wide array of hotels that cater to every need and preference.

1. The Breakers Palm Beach

Located in the upscale town of Palm Beach, The Breakers is a historic luxury resort that has been welcoming guests since 1896. This iconic hotel offers a perfect blend of old-world charm and modern amenities. With its private beach, two championship golf courses, and four oceanfront pools, The Breakers is a paradise for relaxation and recreation.

2. Acqualina Resort & Residences on the Beach

Situated in Sunny Isles Beach, Acqualina Resort is a Forbes Five-Star and AAA Five Diamond hotel that epitomizes luxury. This Mediterranean-inspired resort offers spacious rooms and suites with breathtaking ocean views. Guests can enjoy three oceanfront pools, a world-class spa, and direct access to a pristine private beach.

3. The Ritz-Carlton, Naples

For those seeking a sophisticated retreat, The Ritz-Carlton in Naples is a top choice. This elegant hotel is known for its impeccable service, luxurious accommodations, and stunning Gulf of Mexico views. The resort features two championship golf courses, a full-service spa, and a private beach.

4. Four Seasons Resort Orlando at Walt Disney World Resort

Combining luxury with family-friendly fun, the Four Seasons Resort Orlando is a standout hotel for those visiting the theme park capital of the world. Located within Walt Disney World Resort, this five-star property offers spacious rooms, a tranquil adults-only pool, and a water park with a lazy river and waterslides.

5. The Don CeSar, St. Pete Beach

Known as the "Pink Palace," The Don CeSar is a historic landmark and one of Florida's most iconic hotels. Located on St. Pete Beach, this luxurious resort offers a blend of vintage glamour and modern amenities. Guests can relax by the pool, enjoy water sports on the Gulf of Mexico, or indulge in a treatment at the full-service spa.

6. Faena Hotel Miami Beach

For a trendy and artistic stay, the Faena Hotel Miami Beach is a top choice. This luxurious hotel is part of the Faena District, a vibrant neighborhood known for its art, culture, and entertainment. The hotel features opulent rooms, a stunning pool, and a private beach.

Conclusion

Florida's diverse range of hotels ensures that every traveler can find the perfect accommodation to suit their needs. Whether you're looking for a historic resort, a family-friendly retreat, or a chic urban escape, the Sunshine State has it all. Each of these hotels offers exceptional service, luxurious amenities, and unforgettable experiences, making them some of the best in Florida.
