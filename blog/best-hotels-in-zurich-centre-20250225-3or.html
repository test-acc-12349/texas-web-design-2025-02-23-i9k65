Best Hotels in Zurich Centre

Published on February 23, 2024 • 10 min read

Zurich, the largest city in Switzerland, is renowned for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. As a global financial hub and a popular tourist destination, Zurich offers a wide range of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs. If you're planning a trip to this picturesque city, staying in Zurich Centre ensures you're close to major attractions, shopping districts, and dining options.

1. Baur au Lac

Nestled in a private park near Lake Zurich, Baur au Lac is one of the most luxurious hotels in Zurich Centre. This five-star hotel has been a favorite among discerning travelers since 1844. Its elegant rooms and suites offer breathtaking views of the lake or the Alps, ensuring a memorable stay. The hotel also boasts world-class dining options, including the Michelin-starred Pavillon restaurant.

2. The Dolder Grand

Perched on a hill overlooking Zurich, The Dolder Grand is a blend of historic architecture and modern design. This iconic hotel is just a short drive from Zurich Centre, making it a convenient choice for those who want to stay close to the city while enjoying a tranquil retreat. The Dolder Grand features spacious rooms, a luxurious spa, and two gourmet restaurants.

3. Hotel Storchen

Located in the heart of Zurich's Old Town, Hotel Storchen is a boutique hotel that combines historic charm with contemporary comfort. This family-owned hotel is steps away from the Limmat River and offers stunning views of the city's iconic landmarks. The rooms are elegantly designed, and the hotel's terrace restaurant is a perfect spot to enjoy Swiss cuisine.

4. Park Hyatt Zurich

For those who prefer modern luxury, Park Hyatt Zurich is an excellent choice. Situated near Bahnhofstrasse, one of the world's most exclusive shopping streets, this five-star hotel offers sleek and sophisticated accommodations. The rooms are spacious and feature floor-to-ceiling windows, providing plenty of natural light.

5. Widder Hotel

The Widder Hotel is a unique blend of nine historic townhouses, each with its own character and charm. Located in Zurich's Old Town, this boutique hotel offers a one-of-a-kind experience with its individually designed rooms and suites. The hotel's restaurant, Widder Restaurant, is known for its innovative cuisine and extensive wine list.

6. Hotel Schweizerhof Zürich

Hotel Schweizerhof Zürich is a classic choice for travelers seeking comfort and convenience. Located opposite Zurich's main train station, this hotel is perfect for those who want to explore Zurich Centre and beyond. The rooms are tastefully decorated, and the hotel's restaurant, La Soupière, offers a delightful dining experience.

7. 25hours Hotel Zurich Langstrasse

If you're looking for a trendy and vibrant atmosphere, 25hours Hotel Zurich Langstrasse is the place to be. This design-focused hotel is located in the lively Langstrasse district, known for its eclectic mix of cultures and nightlife. The hotel's rooms are creatively themed, and the rooftop bar offers panoramic views of Zurich.

Conclusion

Zurich Centre is home to some of the finest hotels in Switzerland, catering to a wide range of preferences and budgets. Whether you're looking for historic charm, modern luxury, or a boutique experience, these hotels offer exceptional accommodations and amenities. Staying in Zurich Centre ensures you're at the heart of the action, with easy access to the city's top attractions, dining, and shopping.
