Best Hotels in Paris: A Guide to Luxury and Comfort

Published on February 23, 2024 • 10 min read

Paris, the city of love, the city of lights, and the city of art, is a name that conjures up picturesque sights of glittering Eiffel tower, beautiful gardens of Versailles, and the serene Seine river. The city is not only famous for its artistic appeal and historical richness, but it is also known for its luxury and comfort when it comes to the best hotels.

1. Hôtel Ritz Paris

The legendary Hôtel Ritz Paris, located in the heart of Paris, is synonymous with elegance and luxury. This hotel has been hosting royalty, artists, and writers since it opened in 1898. With its grand décor and high-end services, the Ritz Paris has set a benchmark in the realm of luxury hotels.

2. Le Meurice

Overlooking the palace of Tuileries, Le Meurice offers an excellent view of the city. This hotel is renowned for its Louis XVI inspired décor, which perfectly captures the spirit of 18th-century Versailles. Apart from its historical richness, the hotel houses a two Michelin-starred restaurant managed by the famous chef Alain Ducasse.

3. Four Seasons Hotel George V

The Four Seasons Hotel George V is located near the Champs-Élysées, which is one of the most beautiful avenues in the world. This hotel is renowned for its exquisite floral arrangements, three Michelin-starred restaurants, and wine cellar, housing over 50,000 bottles of wine.

4. The Peninsula Paris

Located in a historical building near the Arc de Triomphe, The Peninsula Paris perfectly fuses its historical charm with modern comfort. The rooftop restaurant, L'Oiseau Blanc, offers a panoramic view of the city. This hotel is often applauded for its thoughtful service and attention to detail.

5. Shangri-La Hotel Paris

Originally a palace built for Napoleon Bonaparte's grandnephew, the Shangri-La Hotel Paris offers breathtaking views of the Eiffel tower and Seine river. This hotel stands out with its impressive architecture, intricate décor, and an esteemed Michelin-starred restaurant.

6. Hôtel Plaza Athénée

The Hôtel Plaza Athénée is located on the prestigious Avenue Montaigne, boasting an impressive frontage decorated with red window awnings and geranium-filled window boxes. This hotel is known for its Dior Institute spa and the famous Alain Ducasse's restaurant.

7. The Hoxton

For those preferring boutique hotels, The Hoxton is an excellent choice. Located in the 2nd arrondissement, it offers stylish rooms with vintage furniture, a trendy bar, and a beautiful courtyard. The hotel's unique design and lively atmosphere make it a favorite among young travelers.

These hotels encapsulate Paris's charm and beauty, offering comfort and luxury to their guests. Each of these hotels is unique in their style and service, ensuring a memorable stay in the city. Thus, whether you are traveling for business or leisure, these hotels in Paris will undoubtedly offer an unparalleled experience of comfort, style, and luxury.
