Best Hotels in Austin City Center

Published on February 23, 2024 • 10 min read

Austin, Texas, is a vibrant city known for its live music scene, eclectic culture, and thriving food scene. Whether you're visiting for business, pleasure, or a bit of both, staying in the heart of the city ensures you're close to all the action.

1. The Driskill

One of the most iconic hotels in Austin, The Driskill, combines historic charm with modern luxury. Built in 1886, this hotel is a landmark in the city and offers a unique blend of old-world elegance and contemporary comfort. Located on 6th Street, The Driskill is steps away from live music venues, restaurants, and bars.

2. JW Marriott Austin

For those who prefer modern luxury, the JW Marriott Austin is a top choice. This upscale hotel is located in the heart of downtown Austin, just a short walk from the Austin Convention Center and the famous South Congress Avenue.

3. Hotel Van Zandt

If you're looking for a boutique experience, Hotel Van Zandt is a must-visit. This stylish hotel is located in the Rainey Street Historic District, known for its trendy bars and food trucks. Hotel Van Zandt offers a more intimate and personalized stay compared to larger chain hotels.

4. Fairmont Austin

Fairmont Austin is a luxurious option for travelers seeking a high-end experience. This hotel is conveniently located near the Austin Convention Center and offers easy access to the city's top attractions.

5. Kimpton Hotel Van Zandt

Another standout in the boutique hotel category is the Kimpton Hotel Van Zandt. Located in the vibrant Rainey Street District, this hotel is known for its lively atmosphere and exceptional service.

6. Hilton Austin

For travelers seeking a reliable and comfortable stay, the Hilton Austin is an excellent choice. Located just steps from the Austin Convention Center, this hotel is perfect for business travelers and convention attendees.

Conclusion

Austin City Center is home to some of the best hotels in the city, catering to a wide range of preferences and budgets. Whether you're looking for historic charm, modern luxury, or a boutique experience, there's a hotel in Austin City Center that's perfect for you.
