Best Hotels in Puerto Vallarta Beachfront

Published on February 23, 2024 • 10 min read

Puerto Vallarta, a stunning coastal city on Mexico's Pacific coast, is renowned for its pristine beaches, vibrant culture, and luxurious accommodations. For travelers seeking the ultimate beachfront experience, the city offers a plethora of hotels that combine breathtaking ocean views with world-class amenities.

1. Garza Blanca Preserve Resort & Spa

Nestled along the shores of Banderas Bay, Garza Blanca Preserve Resort & Spa is a luxurious haven for those seeking tranquility and indulgence. This all-inclusive resort offers spacious suites with private balconies overlooking the turquoise waters of the Pacific Ocean. The hotel boasts a world-class spa, infinity pools, and gourmet dining options that showcase the finest Mexican and international cuisine.

2. Hotel Mousai

If modern elegance and sophistication are what you seek, Hotel Mousai is the ideal destination. This adults-only, all-inclusive hotel is perched on a hillside, offering panoramic views of the ocean and lush jungle. Each suite is a masterpiece of contemporary design, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows, private terraces, and Jacuzzis.

3. Hyatt Ziva Puerto Vallarta

Families and couples alike will find Hyatt Ziva Puerto Vallarta to be a delightful retreat. This all-inclusive beachfront hotel is located in the heart of the Romantic Zone, offering easy access to the city's vibrant nightlife and cultural attractions.

4. Casa Velas

For a more intimate and boutique experience, Casa Velas is a hidden gem in Puerto Vallarta. This adults-only, all-inclusive hotel is surrounded by lush tropical gardens and overlooks a serene beachfront. The hotel offers elegantly designed suites with private plunge pools and outdoor showers.

5. Marriott Puerto Vallarta Resort & Spa

The Marriott Puerto Vallarta Resort & Spa is a family-friendly hotel that combines modern amenities with traditional Mexican charm. Located on the golden sands of Playa de Oro, this hotel offers spacious rooms with private balconies, many of which provide stunning ocean views.

6. Villa Premiere Boutique Hotel & Romantic Getaway

As the name suggests, Villa Premiere is a boutique hotel designed for romance and relaxation. This adults-only, all-inclusive hotel is located on the shores of Banderas Bay, offering direct access to the beach. The hotel features elegantly decorated rooms with ocean views, a serene spa, and a gourmet restaurant that specializes in Mexican cuisine.

Conclusion

Puerto Vallarta's beachfront hotels offer something for everyone, from luxurious all-inclusive resorts to intimate boutique retreats. Whether you're looking for a family-friendly destination, a romantic escape, or a modern haven, these hotels provide the perfect blend of comfort, style, and breathtaking views.
