Best Hotels in Downtown LA

Published on February 23, 2024 • 8 min read

Los Angeles, the City of Angels, is known for its sunny skies, diverse culture, and a thriving entertainment industry. Downtown LA, in particular, holds a prominent place with its mix of modern and historical architecture, restaurants, museums, and nightlife. But one of the most critical aspects to get the best out of your Downtown LA experience is choosing the right place to stay.

1. InterContinental Los Angeles Downtown

The InterContinental is a standout among Downtown LA's hotels. Its towering presence in the city skyline, coupled with its luxurious accommodations, makes it a top choice for visitors. From the hotel, you have a breathtaking panoramic view of the city. Rooms are designed with an elegant modern aesthetic, and you can enjoy a dip in the outdoor rooftop pool or dine in one of several on-site restaurants.

2. The NoMad Hotel Los Angeles

Located in the historical Giannini Building, the NoMad Los Angeles offers a blend of classic European influence and modern comfort. Each room is individually designed with custom furnishings and original artwork. The hotel also features a rooftop pool, two distinctive restaurants, and a cocktail bar.

3. The Ritz-Carlton, Los Angeles

The Ritz-Carlton offers a level of luxury that is hard to beat. This hotel provides magnificent views of the city and comfortable rooms with a sleek design. The hotel has a rooftop pool and a fully-equipped fitness center. The Ritz-Carlton also houses WP24 by Wolfgang Puck, where guests can enjoy a fine dining experience with a view.

4. Hotel Indigo Los Angeles Downtown

Taking inspiration from the 1920s era with a contemporary twist, Hotel Indigo offers a unique stay experience. The rooms are spacious with vibrant designs, and the hotel has an outdoor pool, fitness center, and an on-site restaurant, Metropole Bar+Kitchen. The location is in the heart of downtown, making it easy for guests to explore the cultural highlights of the city.

5. The Standard, Downtown LA

This hotel is known for its stylish and bold design. The Standard offers spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows that offer a spectacular view of the city. The hotel has a rooftop bar, a heated swimming pool, and ping pong tables. It's a perfect choice for those who want to experience LA's vibrant nightlife.

6. Ace Hotel Downtown Los Angeles

Situated in the historic United Artists building, Ace Hotel brings a sense of artistry and creativity to their accommodations. Each room is designed with a modern, minimalist style and equipped with a guitar. The hotel also has a rooftop pool and bar, and a restaurant that specializes in locally sourced ingredients.

All these hotels provide luxurious accommodations that ensure a comfortable and relaxing stay in the heart of LA. Each one has its unique charm and offers different amenities and experiences. Whether you are in Downtown LA for business or leisure, these hotels will definitely elevate your stay. They are not just places to rest after a long day of exploration but are destinations in and of themselves. Undeniably, these are some of the best hotels Downtown LA has to offer.
