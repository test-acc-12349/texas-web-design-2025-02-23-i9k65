Best Hotels in Costa Rica Beachfront

Published on February 23, 2024 • 10 min read

Costa Rica, a tropical paradise known for its lush rainforests, diverse wildlife, and pristine beaches, is a dream destination for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure. For those looking to stay close to the ocean, Costa Rica offers an array of beachfront hotels that combine luxury, comfort, and breathtaking views.

1. Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica at Peninsula Papagayo

Nestled on the stunning Papagayo Peninsula, the Four Seasons Resort is a top-tier choice for travelers seeking luxury and exclusivity. This beachfront hotel offers world-class amenities, including two pristine beaches, a championship golf course, and a state-of-the-art spa.

2. Andaz Costa Rica Resort at Peninsula Papagayo

Another gem on the Papagayo Peninsula, the Andaz Costa Rica Resort is a modern and stylish beachfront hotel that caters to both relaxation and adventure. The resort's design seamlessly blends with its natural surroundings, offering guests a sense of harmony with the environment.

3. The Westin Reserva Conchal

Located on the Guanacaste coast, The Westin Reserva Conchal is an all-inclusive beachfront hotel that promises a stress-free vacation. The resort is set on a sprawling property that includes a pristine beach, a championship golf course, and a luxurious spa.

4. Arenas del Mar Beachfront & Rainforest Resort

For travelers who want to immerse themselves in nature, Arenas del Mar Beachfront & Rainforest Resort in Manuel Antonio is an ideal choice. This eco-friendly hotel is surrounded by lush rainforest and offers direct access to two private beaches.

5. El Mangroove, Autograph Collection

Situated on the Gulf of Papagayo, El Mangroove is a boutique beachfront hotel that offers a unique blend of modern design and natural beauty. The hotel's chic and minimalist aesthetic is complemented by its lush surroundings, creating a tranquil atmosphere.

6. Hotel Punta Islita, Autograph Collection

Located on the Nicoya Peninsula, Hotel Punta Islita is a secluded beachfront hotel that offers a true escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The hotel is set on a hillside, providing stunning views of the Pacific Ocean and the surrounding landscape.

Conclusion

Costa Rica's beachfront hotels offer something for everyone, from luxurious resorts to eco-friendly retreats. Whether you're looking for a romantic escape, a family-friendly vacation, or an adventure-filled getaway, these hotels provide the perfect base for exploring the country's stunning coastline and natural wonders.
