Best Hotels in London

Published on February 23, 2024 • 8 min read

London, the capital of England, is renowned for its rich history, breathtaking landmarks, and vibrant culture. This bustling city is also home to some of the world's most luxurious and beautifully designed hotels. These hotels provide the perfect base for exploring the city's numerous attractions, offering comfort, high-end amenities, and exceptional service.

The Ritz London

The Ritz London, located in the heart of the city, provides an unrivalled luxury experience. Established in 1906, the iconic hotel is a symbol of timeless elegance and glamour. With its lavish Louis XVI-style interiors, it's no wonder that The Ritz has been a favourite choice among celebrities and royalty. Beyond the exquisite decor, The Ritz offers top-notch service and a range of luxurious amenities, including a Michelin-starred restaurant, a sumptuous afternoon tea service, and a private casino.

Claridge's

Situated in Mayfair, one of London's most exclusive areas, Claridge's is an embodiment of timeless elegance. Known for its beautiful Art Deco architecture and lavish interiors, Claridge's combines classic charm with modern comfort. High ceilings, chandeliers, and antique furnishings adorn the rooms, providing a luxurious setting for guests. The hotel also boasts a selection of renowned restaurants and bars, including the Michelin-starred restaurant 'Fera at Claridge's'.

The Savoy

The Savoy is one of London's most iconic hotels. Overlooking the River Thames, the hotel has a rich history dating back to 1889. From royalty to celebrities, The Savoy has hosted some of the world's most notable figures. The hotel's Edwardian and Art Deco styles create a sense of grandeur and sophistication. The hotel's amenities do not disappoint either, from its award-winning restaurants - Gordon Ramsay's Savoy Grill and Kaspar's at The Savoy, to the legendary American Bar, the oldest cocktail bar in London.

The Connaught

Located in the heart of Mayfair, The Connaught offers a blend of traditional elegance and contemporary style. Its stunning Victorian architecture and beautiful interior design create a warm and inviting atmosphere. The hotel features an array of high-end amenities, including a world-class spa, a Michelin-starred restaurant, and a renowned bar. The Connaught's splendid service and luxury make it one of the best hotels in London.

Shangri-La Hotel at The Shard

London's skyline is dominated by The Shard, the tallest building in Western Europe. Here, on the 34th to 52nd floors, you will find the Shangri-La Hotel. Offering panoramic views of the city, the hotel provides a unique luxury experience. The rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows, marble bathrooms, and Asian-inspired decor. The hotel's amenities include a sky pool, a renowned restaurant, and a cocktail bar.

These hotels represent just a taste of what London has to offer. Each establishment is unique in its own way, providing luxurious accommodation, high-end amenities, and exceptional service. While these hotels are on the pricier side, the experience they offer is worth every penny. Whether it is for a special occasion or simply to experience the city in style, these hotels offer the ultimate luxury experience in the heart of one of the world's greatest cities.
