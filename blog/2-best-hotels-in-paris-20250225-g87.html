Best Hotels in Paris

Published on February 23, 2024 • 6 min read

Paris, the city of love, fashion, and gastronomy, is one of the world's most visited tourist destinations. The city offers countless attractions, including the iconic Eiffel Tower, the stunning Louvre Museum, and the charming streets of Montmartre. However, to truly soak up the Parisian atmosphere, tourists need to stay in a comfortable and cozy hotel. In this article, we will explore some of the best hotels in Paris where you can rest after a long day of sightseeing.

1. The Ritz Paris

Firstly, no conversation about Parisian hotels can start without mentioning the Ritz Paris. This legendary hotel is situated in Place Vendôme, in the heart of Paris. The Ritz is synonymous with luxury, opulence, and impeccable service. Its rooms and suites are tastefully decorated in classic French style, blending elegance with modern amenities. The hotel also houses several restaurants and bars, including the famed Bar Hemingway.

2. Le Meurice

Next on the list is Le Meurice, located in the 1st arrondissement, right in front of the Tuileries Garden. Le Meurice's design is inspired by the grandeur of Versailles. Its rooms have a unique blend of classic French decor and modern comfort. The hotel's restaurant, led by renowned chef Alain Ducasse, has two Michelin stars, making it a paradise for foodies.

3. Shangri-La Hotel Paris

Formerly the home of Napoleon Bonaparte's grand-nephew, Shangri-La Hotel Paris offers a breathtaking view of the Eiffel Tower. The rooms and suites are decorated in both European and Asian styles, reflecting the hotel's Eastern heritage. The hotel also has a two-Michelin-starred restaurant, L'Abeille, which serves exquisite French cuisine.

4. Four Seasons Hotel George V

Situated only steps from the Champs-Elysées, the Four Seasons Hotel George V is an Art Deco landmark. The hotel offers spacious and luxurious rooms and suites with picturesque views of the city or the hotel's inner courtyard. Besides, its three restaurants, each with Michelin stars, serve exceptional French and Mediterranean cuisine.

5. Hôtel Plaza Athénée

Last but not least, Hôtel Plaza Athénée, located on the famous Avenue Montaigne, is surrounded by luxury boutiques and theaters. The hotel's rooms and suites are decorated in Art Deco style or a classic Louis XVI style. The hotel's restaurant, Alain Ducasse au Plaza Athénée, has been awarded three Michelin stars, presenting the best of haute cuisine.

To conclude, Paris, with its diverse array of luxury establishments, offers numerous options for tourists in search of the best hotels. Whether you are an art lover, a food enthusiast, or just someone wanting to enjoy Parisian charm, these hotels provide an exceptional experience. Remember, Paris is not just a city; it's a world. And these hotels will ensure that your stay in this world is as enchanting as the city itself.
